Beauty calls, who will listen and see

My name is Jacob Albert and I am a self-taught amateur Photographer. I got my photography start in 2021 with a second hand DSLR and kit zoom lens. I have since made the switch to mirrorless and settled on the Sony eco-system.

I wouldn't say that photoraphy or anything creative comes easy to me but I often feel the need express emotion in ways logic cannot. Simply saying something isn't sufficient, it cannot compare to mutual experience. I feel similarly of the subjects I photograph. There is emotion all around us that often goes overlooked. There are stories being told that no one stops to listen to. I hope to capture some of these and share them with you. It's said the eyes are the windows to the soul and I hope your soul is touched when you see the stories I present.
